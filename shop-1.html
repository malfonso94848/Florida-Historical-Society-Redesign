    Discovering A.S.J. Allen: A Story of Skinfolk, Kinfolk, and Village Folk
    By: James Smith

    Growing up, Alonzo Felder heard just a few stories about his great-
    grandfather A.S.J. Allen. In this book the author shares his process,
    providing guidance to others seeking to discover the stories of their
    ancestors. The Rev A.S.J. Allen was a respected African American
    community leader in Alachua County, Florida.
