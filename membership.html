    The Florida Historical Society is a 501(c)(3) not-for-profit organization. We depend upon Membership support, along with grant funding and sponsorships, to maintain our facilities and present our many educational outreach projects and programs. Your Membership support is much appreciated!

    Membership Benefits Include:

    All members receive our newsletter, The Society Report, a 10% discount on FHS Press Books and at FHS Bookstore; and early notice of special events, including our Annual Meeting!
    All FHS Membership run for one year, beginning the month payment is received.
    Memberships are non refundable.
